About Us

Legacy of Learning Since 1986

At the heart of GYAN NIKETAN is a dream—one envisioned by our esteemed founder, Padma Shree Acharya Kunal Kishore, IPS and Mrs. Anita Kumnal, Philanthropist & Educationist, whose passion for education laid the foundation of this institution under the aegis of M.V.V.Nyas. With a firm belief that every child deserves access to quality learning and a nurturing environment, they established Gyan Niketan in the year 1986 with the goal of shaping not just educated minds, but responsible and compassionate citizens. The first batch appeared in the year 1990 through CBSE, New Delhi ad created an excellence at National Level.
